split

The split() method will seperate items in a string. 

                            
string = "Superman,Clark Kent,can fly,super strength"
print(string)
name,alias,power1,power2 = split(",")
print(f"{name} is also known as {alias} and has the 
        powers {power1} and {power2}.")
                            
                        

Superman,Clark Kent,can fly,super strength
Superman is also known as Clark Kent and has the 
powers can fly and super strength.
